This is a custom project made to increase productivity during the scam investigations. I would like to confirm that I was not hired or appointed by any Admin or SI Department Leader in HiddenDevs to make this project. 

It was meant to be primarily serve me in my investigation procedures but due to people having interest in it, I am releasing an open-sourced version of my project for them.


# Overview

It's remarkable to see how HiddenDevs SIs handle almost 50-100 cases a month through their sheer determination and hardwork. As a TSI, the biggest problem which I faced was managing the information, which was... a terrible experience. 

Don't get me wrong, a lot of SIs in the past have done incredible work like this. But what can I say, I am going to be a 3rd CS Major soon. I need something which helps me manage my stuff easily and efficiently. And hence, took the opportunity to make an application for the ease :)


# About the app

This SI Toolkit is built on Electron Framework by OpenJS using HTML5 for the struture, CSS3 for the design and ES6+ & Node.js for the backend. It doesn't need any internet requirement, cloud connection or administrator permissions. Everything is saved locally in your drive.

# Features

- **ID Tracker:** Helps in managing the sheer amount of Discord User IDs and Roblox IDs
- **Message Templates Clipboard:** Helps you in drafting messages to be sent to victims and suspects. Easily customize the templates according to your needs.
- **Evidence Media Player:** Play the evidence video in the in-built HTML5 Player. Take screenshots on the go and use the In-Built Annotation Canvas to redact any sensitive information. Easily export the screenshots into a folder.
- **Video Reverser Engine:** Tired of those people sending evidence from bottom to top? Use this to invert back the video!!
- **Ban Log Template Generator:** Easily make the ban log template(s). Very useful when making 20-30 ban logs (if you're unlucky enough lmao)
- **Notes and Checklist Section:** Use the in-built Notes area for anything (idk I thought it would be cool but not sure about the actual use case). A checklist is also present so you can track every step before heading to the next one!
- **Themes:** Who doesn't like themes?
